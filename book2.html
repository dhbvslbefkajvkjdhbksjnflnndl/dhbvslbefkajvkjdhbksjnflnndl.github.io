Today I saw
a man sleeping
on the pavement,

“wot do u feel”
was written, in pen
across his arm.

Sadness?
Or maybe relief.

I don’t know.

“I’m getting
some coffee.
Want some?”

“Naaaaaaaaaaaah
I just had one.
Thanks anyway.”

I watched him
fall back asleep
on the pavement,

and felt tired.

a poem about a bare

you would have said

When I Talk About My Twitter Feed, I Refer to You as “My Friend”

When walking,
sometimes I feel
groups approach
from behind.

I slow down.

I briefly imagine
I’m their friend.

I listen.
& nod along.

the soap they use kills the fish in the harbour

i asked for a matcha smoothie
made with soya, to go.
the lady behind me
told me that the packaging
was not recyclable.

I sat down and opened
my MacBook.

?? Doesn’t she know about the soap?

????

Men who lift
their gut
up over
a belt strap
while looking
in your eyes

earnestly
telling you
their jeans size
hasn't changed
for 15 years.


I renewed my gym
membership recently.

I’m really excited
to get back into it.

You Were Just Here

A type of loneliness
where the only
human contact
is the still-warm seat
in public restrooms.

Send Nudes

Today I saw someone
ask for photos of dogs,
or kittens.

They were feeling
sad. They needed
help.

I started to type:
"have you tried Google?”,

but I realised

that they just wanted to know
that people out there cared

& I wasn't sure I did.
